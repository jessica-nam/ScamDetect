What is GoPhish and why is it impressive?

GoPhish is a cutting-edge tool that uses advanced machine learning algorithms and natural language processing to detect email and SMS scams. With this powerful tool, you can easily copy and paste any suspicious message into the text box and receive an instant analysis of its authenticity.

What is phishing? Why is it dangerous?

Phishing attacks are a serious threat to individuals and organizations alike. These attacks involve tricking people into providing sensitive information such as login credentials, credit card numbers, or social security numbers.
